label codex_anomalies:
    show d10c-62-x3
    with w33slow
    window hide
    nvl clear
    Nar "Judging by the first mentions of anomalies that appeared almost 400 years ago, it is commonly believed that the timeline of the first sightings coincides with the event that shaped the modern world."
    Nar "One might believe that anomalies, which we define as phenomena and events that cannot be explained through our knowledge of physics, occurred even before the \"End of days\" as some call it."
    Nar "Unidentified flying objects or unexplainable natural sightings in shapes that were not readily explainable - I have read about those things from documents of the old world. However, it feels wrong to believe that these things really relate to what we experience in our current world."
    Nar "I have read about what scholars now call the age of information and disinformation. A time in which the masses were distracted from what was in front of them, through the spreading of information that was often represented in a misleading way or entirely fictional."
    Nar "Yet, what this world faces can not be confused with fairytales, conspiracy theories or other made up stories."
    Nar "I strongly believe that anomalies are a direct result of the catastrophe that was unleashed on the world roughly 400 years ago. Those who survived found themselves prey of a cruel game nature started to play."
    Nar "Elements that once were bound by the rules of physics were left unchained and bouncing around without any reason or pattern. Heat, gravity and electricity emerging from the ground or manifesting in the air, reshaping as they please."
    Nar "Every Stalker has heard of these stories - whether it's burnt fuzzes, tesla anomalies or whirlgigs - their behaviour follows only their internal logic, inscrutable and unknown."
    Nar "Never extinguishing torches, found in ruins long forgotten, vegetation growing in places barren for decades; the documentation is clear, and relies on the testimony of our forefathers who have battled these anomalies just as we do."
    Nar "Anomalies can occur everywhere, but the highest concentrations of them has been observed in what we call \"Zones\"; specific locations, utterly diverse and ever changing."
    Nar "To quote one of the earliest records I have found reporting on the Zones: \"It is likely that emissions, a kind of sudden outbursts of energy, are causing the creation and modification of anomalies.\""
    Nar "In rare cases, I understand, anomalies can cause a \"fossilization\" of the surrounding objects and elements, to create so called \"artifacts\", as they are known by Stalkers. But I have yet to encounter such a rare circumstance."
    Nar "There are other phenomena that have yet to be explained, such as the red mist of Old Cairo, or the cursed storms in its immediate surroundings."
    Nar "To what extent they are connected to the comparatively small anomalies that can be encountered in more remote Zones, I don't know."
    Nar "I guess that the reason why no one speaks of the mist in Old Cairo as an \"anomaly\" is because unlike those, who are subject to the passage of time, the mist in Old Cairo never fades and has been there as far as living memory and records go."
    jump zone_codex_index

label codex_spirits:
    show d10c-62-x4
    with w33slow
    window hide
    nvl clear
    Nar "Every Stalker has heard legends. They form the core of our education, after all. Some are more factual, and tell about the origins of the Zones, supposed to be found somewhere in what was known as Eastern Europe, long before our time."
    Nar "Some tales are about the life before the birth of the new world. Some even tell stories about events that cyclically present themselves throughout time, stories about spirits returning to this world, or worse, never leaving it."
    Nar "While I have not personally witnessed any similar event, I have been fortunate enough to see anomalies with my own eyes. How farfetched can a story about spirits be, I ask myself, when such portents are real and tangible?"
    Nar "Ancient recordings going back entire civilisations, tell us about the original Egyptians, in whose image the city-state of Zeta was founded. It appears they had some knowledge of the ways of life and of what's beyond."
    Nar "The scriptures of Ancient Egypt talk about the Gods, the structure of the cosmos and how our lives will be one day judged, our souls measured by the goddess Maat. The soul was understood to exist in three parts; the Ka, the Ba, and the Akh."
    Nar "The Ka, the soul that survives our death, would linger and wait for the goddess to pronounce its fate; the Ba, the messenger side of our spirit, would keep the connection between the spirit world and the body."
    Nar "The Akh on the other hand, was said to be an effective, manifested spirit, which could still influence events in this world. The Akh and the Ka were believed to need a preserved body and tomb in order to exist."
    Nar "Comparing these scriptures with the stories of the spirits encountered by countless Stalkers, it's understandable why legends about Akh still lingering around in this world, are so popular and widely known."
    Nar "One part of me hopes to never encounter such a phenomenon, and for it to be just be an expression of the trauma suffered by Stalkers. Another part of me is curious."
    Nar "If the Akh exist, are they conscious? Do they interact with the outside world, or do they create their own?"
    jump zone_codex_index
